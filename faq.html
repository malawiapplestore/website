Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Are your products genuine Apple products?

Yes, all our products are 100% genuine Apple products. We source directly from authorized distributors and guarantee authenticity on every item we sell.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty. The warranty period varies depending on the product. Please contact us for specific warranty information on any product.

Do you deliver across Malawi?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities and towns across Malawi. Delivery times may vary depending on your location. Contact us for specific delivery information to your area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers. For large purchases, we may also offer installment plans. Contact us for more details.

Do you offer trade-in or swap deals?

Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer swap deals on eligible devices. The value of your trade-in depends on the device model, condition, and current market value. Contact us to get a quote for your device.

Can I check if a product is in stock before visiting?

Yes, you can check product availability through our website or contact us via WhatsApp or phone. We'll let you know immediately if the item is in stock.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide technical support for all products purchased from us. Our team can help with setup, troubleshooting, and general usage questions. Contact us for assistance.

What should I do if I receive a defective product?

If you receive a defective product, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund according to our warranty policy. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I reserve a product and pick it up later?

Yes, we can reserve products for you. Contact us with the product details, and we'll hold it for you. A deposit may be required for reservations.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through WhatsApp, phone, email, or by visiting our store. Our contact information is available on the Contact page. We're here to help!
